About Our Mission

PawsFeed Pune is dedicated to providing nutritious meals to street dogs in our city. We believe every dog deserves access to food, regardless of their circumstances.

Founded in 2025, we've grown from a small local initiative to a city-wide network of volunteers and supporters across Pune.

With your help, we've served over 150 meals to street dogs in the past year alone.
